Let's tell you a bit about us


Who Are We?

King's College London students passionate about technology and building tech products and companies.

What Do We Do?

Connect students and industry. Organise events and workshops to develop skills and expertise relevant to current tech trends.

How To Join?

Sign up using the form above to become a member and subscribe to our newsletter.

It's All About The Events.

Events are what make us. We work with some great people like Codecademy and Facebook to bring you awesome hackathons and tech talks. We also hold student-run workshops to teach you about the latest technologies. Complete beginner? Don't worry, we do stuff for you too!

We're working with some really cool people.

We bring leaders in the tech industry to campus. Whether it's an awesome startup like Codecademy to a tech giant like Microsoft, there are a lot of cool stories and opportunities to make the most of.
